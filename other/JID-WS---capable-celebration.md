## About

WhatsApp AI Assistant Bot is a Node.js-based WhatsApp bot that integrates with OpenAI's Assistant API using BuilderBot technology. It enables automated, intelligent conversations on WhatsApp, configurable via your own OpenAI Assistant and environment variables.

Hosting WhatsApp AI Assistant Bot on Railway allows you to deploy a production-ready conversational bot for WhatsApp with minimal setup. The bot supports custom flows, intelligent responses, and can be tailored to your business or personal needs by adjusting environment variables and assistant configuration. Railway handles the infrastructure, so you can focus on building and improving your bot logic, not on server management.
