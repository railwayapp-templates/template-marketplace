# Deploy AirMultiLocks on Railway

Deploy AirMultiLocks with Railway

[![Deploy on Railway](https://railway.com/button.svg)](https://railway.com/deploy/wide-red)

## About

Automatically syncs your Airbnb guest codes to all your door locks — no manual copying, no missed doors.

Click the button above — Railway creates a free service for you automatically.
Visit your Railway URL — the setup wizard walks you through connecting your locks (takes about 2 minutes).
Save your configuration variables — the wizard shows you which values to paste into Railway so your settings survive future updates.
