## About

### Basic Sveltekit and Pocketbase Starter with Docker and Svelte 5 + Sveltekit 2.

Sveltekit comes with:
- Typescript
- eslint & prettier
- TailwindCSS
- Husky for pre-commit hooks

If you have any questions regarding setup or you have trouble starting this just email me: jarmo@exord.de

Happy coding!
