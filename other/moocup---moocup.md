## About

moocup is visual screenshot editor, where you simply drop your image and style it. moocup is designed to be quick and not waste your time, a base style is always applied so you can be quick with it. plenty options are provided so you customize to however you wish. realtime canvas updates as you do changed to your image giving you instant visual feedback.

hosting moocup gives you instant access to complete, comprehensive screenshot editor with a realtime canvas.
moocup is designed to be a offilne-first app, so even if you do not have access to internet, you can still enjoy moocup offline. hosting moocup gives you flexibility of having a personal copy that you can modify and use however you wish.
