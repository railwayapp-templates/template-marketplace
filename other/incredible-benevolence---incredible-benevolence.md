## About

- **Deploy:** Assemble the layout on a stable 4x8 ft table or workbench in a dedicated hobby space.
- **Host:** Display the completed layout at home, local clubs, or model train exhibitions with proper lighting and space for viewers.

- Hosting involves setting up the layout for personal enjoyment or public viewing, ensuring all tracks are functional and scenery is intact. It may require a temporary setup with portable tables or a permanent installation in a basement or garage, depending on space availability.
