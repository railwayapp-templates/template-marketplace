## About

TechGuru Contact Form Service

 A simple, ready-to-deploy Flask-based contact form microservice. Perfect for quickly adding contact functionality to your website, hosted effortlessly using Docker or Railway.

Key Features:

- One-click deployment via Railway
- Customizable Flask app for contact forms
- Dockerized for easy local development or cloud hosting
- Free and open-source (MIT License)

Prerequisites:

- Railway account for deployment
- Docker for local deployment (optional)
- Basic Flask knowledge (optional for customization)

Usage Instructions:

Simply click the 'Deploy on Railway' button, and Railway will take care of the rest. Customize the contact form in main.py and you’re good to go!
