## About

dark-wild is a modern web application framework for building dynamic websites and APIs with minimal configuration.

Deploying dark-wild on Railway is automatic. Railway detects your project, installs dependencies, and handles SSL, domains, and scaling. Just connect your GitHub repo and Railway does the rest.
