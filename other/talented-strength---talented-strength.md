## About

Deploying your project means making it available online so others can access it. Hosting is the service that allows your application or website to be stored and served over the internet.

Hosting refers to the infrastructure and services required to store your application files and make them accessible via the web. There are various types of hosting such as shared hosting, VPS (Virtual Private Server), cloud hosting, and dedicated servers. The choice depends on your project size, traffic, and performance needs.
