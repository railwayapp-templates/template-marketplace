# Deploy Botpress on Railway

Botpress is the ultimate platform for building next-generation chatbots.

[![Deploy on Railway](https://railway.com/button.svg)](https://railway.com/template/yEID2r)

## About

After deploying, click on the main node, go to "Settings", scroll to the "Networking" section and click on "Custom Domain".

Select the port you need (:4000 for the Botpress studio) and click on "Generate Domain". Ideally, you should connect a domain for every single port.

After that, configure your Admin account and you're done!

## What gets deployed

| Service | Source | Type |
|---------|--------|------|
| Botpress | `botpress/server` | Web service |

## Configuration

- **Networking:** Public domain with automatic HTTPS
