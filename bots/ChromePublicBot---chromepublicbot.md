# Deploy ChromePublicBot on Railway

Automated leveraged ETF trading signals executed on your Webull account.

[![Deploy on Railway](https://railway.com/button.svg)](https://railway.com/deploy/chromepublicbot)

## About

ChromePublicBot polls the ChromeSignals signal API and automatically executes leveraged ETF trades on your personal Webull account. Signals are generated by a backtested
  mean reversion strategy covering 9 tickers including TQQQ, SOXL, and UPRO.

  ## About Hosting ChromePublicBot

  The bot runs as a lightweight Python service that checks for new trading signals every 15 seconds during market hours. When a signal arrives, it places a market order on
  your Webull account and sets a -2% hard stop loss. Risk settings like position size and max simultaneous trades sync automatically from your ChromeSignals dashboard with no
  redeployment needed. Your Webull API keys are stored exclusively on your Railway account. ChromeSignals never accesses them.

  ## Common Use Cases

  - Automatically execute leveraged ETF entry and exit signals on Webull
  - Monitor real-time trade activity alongside Telegram alerts
  - Adjust risk settings from the ChromeSignals dashboard without touching Railway

  ## Dependencies for ChromePublicBot Hosting

  - ChromeSignals Auto-Trade subscription at thechromesignals.com
  - Webull account with API access enabled

  ### Deployment Dependencies

  - [ChromeSignals Dashboard](https://www.thechromesignals.com/app/autotrade) — get your API key and manage settings
  - [Webull OpenAPI](https://www.webull.com) — enable developer API access in the Webull app
