## About

# MostLikelyTo Bot

Simple Most-Likely-To minigame for your community, provides slash command `/most-likely-to` which responds with a "who's most likely to" question. You and your imaginary friends then reply with whoever they believe is most likely to do whatever was posed in the question.

## What gets deployed

| Service | Source | Type |
|---------|--------|------|
| MostLikelyTo | [FraglyG/mostlikelyBot](https://github.com/FraglyG/mostlikelyBot) | Worker |
