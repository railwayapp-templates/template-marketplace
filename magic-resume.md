## About

Magic Resume is an open-source, AI-powered resume builder built with Next.js and Tailwind CSS. It features real-time preview, auto-save to browser storage, and easy export of resumes as PDF or JSON config files for later editing and reuse.

Hosting Magic Resume is straightforward and requires minimal configuration. No database or persistent volume is needed since all data is stored client-side in the browser's localStorage. The app runs entirely on the frontend, making it lightweight, fast, and easy to scale. Deployment typically completes in minutes, giving you a public URL for your personal hosted resume builder.
