## About

Shipping a Kotlin Spring app is easy with Gradle and Railway. Just add the necessary packages to your build.gradle file and you're good to go! Whether you need database connectivity, RESTful APIs, or any other functionality, Gradle makes it simple to manage dependencies and package your app for deployment. With Gradle's powerful build system, you can focus on developing your app and trust that Gradle will handle the heavy lifting of packaging and shipping.
