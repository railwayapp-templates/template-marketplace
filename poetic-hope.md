## About

Hosting poetic-hope on Railway makes your app publicly accessible with zero setup hassle. Railway automatically builds your project using your package.json, installs dependencies, and runs your start script inside a secure container. This way, your app is instantly live on a public URL that you can share with friends.
You don’t need to manage servers, Nginx, or Docker manually — Railway takes care of scaling, redeployments, and logs. All you need to do is push code to GitHub or connect your repo to Railway.
