Haptic is a new local-first &amp; privacy-focused, open-source home for your markdown notes. It's minimal, lightweight, efficient and aims to have _all you need and nothing you don't_.

If you'd like to learn more about Haptic, why it's being built, what its goals are and how it differs from all the other markdown editors out there, you can read more about it [here](https://haptic.md/app).

Haptic is currently still in active development.
