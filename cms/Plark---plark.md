## About

[Plark](https://plark.com) is a self-hosted website builder that makes creating and deploying dynamic sites effortless. It’s lightweight, runs on SQLite, and gives you full control over your content and hosting.

## What gets deployed

| Service | Source | Type |
|---------|--------|------|
| Plark | `plarkinc/plark` | Web service |

## Environment variables

| Variable | Default |
| --------- | ------- |
| `PORT` | 80 |

## Configuration

- **Networking:** Public domain with automatic HTTPS
- **Volume:** `/var/data`
